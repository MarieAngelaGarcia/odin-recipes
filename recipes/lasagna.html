Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, meat sauce, and béchamel sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese (ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan)
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Herbs (basil, oregano)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Brown the ground beef with garlic and onion in a pan.
  3. Add tomato sauce and herbs, simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Cook the pasta sheets according to package instructions.
  5. Layer pasta, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
